LOST FOREST

Families bring a waste-free picnic to enjoy at the Garden, or they can purchase picnic fare when they arrive. The Garden provides acres of lush summer landscape as well as popcorn, ice cream, face painters, balloon artists, a DJ, and crafts.

Picnic guests can arrive at the Garden anytime after 5 pm. Picnic box pickup is anytime between 5 and 8 pm. Picnic guests can stay and enjoy the Garden until it closes at 10 pm.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is to the west of the main entrance next to the AMPHITHEATRE.
